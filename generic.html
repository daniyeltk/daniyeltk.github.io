The youth of BPMI are a vibrant generation — a chosen generation, a burning generation, a Joshua generation. We are not called to stand at the borders and only look at the promises of God from afar. We are called to possess the land. We are the generation that will not only see with our eyes, but will also enter with our feet. Though we are based in the great city of Frankfurt, our vision is far beyond borders. Heaven has entrusted us with a divine mandate: *“Go into all the world and preach the Gospel to every creature.”* This is not just a mission — this is the Great Commission. We are raising young men and women who are passionate for Jesus, filled with the Holy Spirit, fearless in faith, and ready to carry the fire of revival to the nations. God is calling this generation to rise. To move. To burn. To preach. To heal. To disciple. To transform lives through the power of the Gospel. You are warmly welcome to be part of what God is doing through BPMI Youth. This is more than a fellowship, it is a family, a mission and a divine assignment. If your heart longs for more of God, if you desire to grow spiritually, impact lives, and walk boldly in purpose, then your place is here. The time is now. The harvest is ready. And this generation will arise and answer the call.
