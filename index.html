Founder: Bishop Dr. Paul Owusu Tabiri

Bishop Dr. Paul Owusu Tabiri served as the first President and Field Representative of the Full Gospel Business Men's Fellowship in the Brong Ahafo Region. In 1988, God called him into full-time Evangelistic and Healing Ministry as a Prophet, Evangelist and Apostle. While on a business trip to the United States, he received this divine calling. He returned to Ghana and founded Bethel Prayer Camp on July 7, 1990, with seven people under the Church of Pentecost leading to Bethel Prayer Ministry International in 1995.
